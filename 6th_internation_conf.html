6th International Conference on Translational Research:

Impact of Post-COVID-19 Pandemic on Society, Healthcare System and Bench to Bed Research

About the conference

"6th International Conference on Translational Research: Impact of Post-COVID-19 Pandemic on Society, Healthcare System and Bench to Bed Research” will be held from 17-18th December, 2021 at KIIT Deemed to be University, Bhubaneswar, India. Onset of COVID-19 pandemic has pushed scientific community beyond their limits to perform translational research in the area of disease diagnostics and drug discovery to vaccine development. This conference will discuss the effect of COVID-19 on translational research worldwide along with other scientific themes. Many eminent researchers will present their exciting work on hybrid mode as Plenary and Invited lectures and short oral presentation. Students and post-doctoral fellows will present their findings using hybrid mode as posters and the extraordinary works will be recognized in the form of short oral presentations and poster awards.

Jointly Organized by:
KIIT Deemed to be University, Bhubaneswar,
India Indian Society of Translational Research (ISTR), New Delhi , India
